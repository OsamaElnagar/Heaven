# HEAVEN — نظام إدارة رحلات الحج والعمرة

**HEAVEN** is a Laravel + Filament admin system built for Egyptian Hajj & Umrah travel companies to replace paper and Excel workflows.

## What it does

- Manages **clients** (pilgrims), their passports, mahram info, and medical notes
- Handles **packages** (Hajj/Umrah), **trips**, **hotels**, and **room assignments**
- Tracks the full **booking lifecycle** from inquiry to completion
- Records **payments** in installments and generates client statements
- Monitors **visa status** per pilgrim with bulk embassy submission support
- Manages **employees**, **suppliers**, and **trip expenses**

## Stack

`Laravel v13` · `Filament v5` · `Livewire v4` · `Alpine.js` · `Tailwind CSS` · `MySQL, sqlite for local`

## Who uses it

Internal staff only — sales, operations, and accounting teams via the Filament admin panel.
A lightweight public-facing site (package listings + booking inquiry + booking tracker) serves clients directly.
